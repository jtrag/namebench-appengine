namebench results

This site provides DNS performance data which has been submitted by users of namebench DNS benchmarking software. For privacy reasons, namebench only uploads details on a predefined list of 10 hosts, and masks internal IP addresses before uploading. To share your own results, download the namebench 1.3-alpha, and run:

./namebench.py -u
