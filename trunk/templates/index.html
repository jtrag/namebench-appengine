namebench results sharing

This site allows you to access DNS benchmark data which has been submitted by the namebench experimental sharing feature. For privacy reasons, namebench only uploads details on a predefined list of 10 hostnames, and all internal IP's are masked before uploading.

If you would like to contribute results, please download the namebench 1.3-alpha build, and invoke namebench with:

./namebench.py -u
